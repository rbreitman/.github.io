More about Roberto

Basic Bio:

In 1998 Roberto was born in Rio de Janeiro Brazil where he would live with his parents and 2 brothers for the first 18 years of his life. There he attended the American School of Rio were he participated in school musicals as a sax player, and played on the football team. Having finished high-school, Roberto moved to Medford Massachusetts int eh United States to attend Tufts university, where he is a junior pursuing a double major in Computer Science and International Relations.

Football Career:

Like every young Brazilian boy, Roberto once dreamed of being a footballer. This is how he failed.

Roberto started footballing at Zero Esporte, a world renown after-school program. At 11 he transitioned to his school team at the American School of Rio, immediately making a big impact as 3rd alternate goalkeeper. By 14 he had fought his way to 2nd alternate, a position he would hold to the end of his middle school career. In Highschool Roberto made an imediate splash, as an injury to the trying keeper have a Roberto a shot at the starting job. Roberto promptly pushed that shot away from the goal as a good goalkeeper should, returning to the bench just 2 weeks later. Following a slow assent to starting keeper, Roberto was forced to hang his jersey and leave the Rio leagues of his youth. Today Roberto is a doubtless starter in the Tufts university Intermural league, where he has built a reputation under the nickname “that Brazilian dude”. The future is bright.
